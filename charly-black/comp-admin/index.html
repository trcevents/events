How the Comp Ticket System Works

A quick read before you sign in below — this is the whole flow, start to finish, so you know what happens after you hand someone a link.

  1. 1. Sign in and grab your link. Once you're signed in, you'll see a box called "Your Referral Link." That link is yours — it already knows your name, so whoever fills it out doesn't have to guess who sent them.
  2. 2. Someone asks you for tickets? Send them your link. They fill out their own name, email, and phone — you don't have to do it for them.
  3. 3. They choose free, sell, or both. Up to 2 free tickets to keep, and up to 10 tickets to sell to start (they can always ask for more once they're moving them). The form shows them what selling is worth — see below.
  4. 4. You and Stephen both get an email the moment they submit. Nothing to do here — it's automatic. That's your heads-up that a request is waiting.
  5. 5. Stephen reviews it and sets up their access in Ticket Tailor. He builds their allocation and sends them a code by email — that's what lets them actually claim free tickets or sell.
  6. 6. They're live. Free tickets are theirs to give away. Sold tickets earn them real money — and you helped make that happen.
Why selling is worth it to them: they keep 70% of every ticket for their first 10 sold, and 50% on every ticket after that, up to 50 total. Worth mentioning when you hand out the link — it's real money, not just a favor.
Someone asks you in person instead of online? No problem. Once you're signed in, click "+ Log a Request" and fill it in yourself on their behalf — same process, same review, from there.
