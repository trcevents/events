Strong. Proud. Connected.

St. Catherine Link-Up brings together Chicagoland's community from three of St. Catherine parish's proudest areas — Linstead, Spanish Town, and Portmore — for a full day of 90s dancehall nostalgia, headlined by Suge — Don Dadda. One parish, one link, one community, stronger together.

Free entry from noon until midnight, with a $20 cover after midnight for those keeping the vibe going late.

Pre-ordered food available on-site — Jerky Jerk & Tropical Jerk
